The Problem

Everything a CSM
needs.
In one place.

From the moment before you join a call to the follow-up that lands in your customer's inbox — Mileo handles the work that disappears between meetings.

01
Signal Dashboard

See exactly what needs your attention today — at-risk accounts, unanswered questions, overdue follow-ups — organized by urgency, not alphabetically.

02
Meeting Intelligence

Prep brief before you join. Live guidance during the call. Automatic follow-up email and action items when you hang up. All three stages, handled.

03
Simulate

Before you build a feature or send a message, test it against your real customers. Mileo shows how each account would react — using their actual words from past conversations, not AI guesses.

04
Team Intelligence

One CSM meeting automatically becomes briefings for Product, Marketing, Ops, and Sales. Each team gets only what's relevant to them. No manual handoffs.
