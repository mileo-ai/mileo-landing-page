Mileo Zoom Integration Guide

Follow these instructions to connect, use, and remove the Mileo app from your Zoom account.

Adding the App

To securely connect Mileo with your Zoom account, follow these simple steps:

  1. Log in to your Zoom account.
  2. Navigate to the Zoom App Marketplace.
  3. Search for Mileo and click on the app icon.
  4. Click the Add button.
  5. Review the requested permissions and click Authorize to allow Mileo to securely access the necessary data for generating meeting insights.
  6. You will be redirected back to the Mileo web platform where your Zoom integration will now show as "Connected."

Having trouble? Please contact our support team at support@mileo.co.

Usage

Mileo will automatically ingest cloud recording transcripts from your Zoom meetings and organize them into your contact profiles.

Removing the App

If you no longer wish to use Mileo with your Zoom account, you can remove the integration at any time from the Zoom Marketplace.

How to remove Mileo:

  1. Log in to your Zoom account and navigate to the Zoom App Marketplace.
  2. Click Manage >> Added Apps or search for the "Mileo" app.
  3. Select the Mileo app.
  4. Click Remove.

Implications of Removal

Mileo will no longer be able to sync your Zoom recordings.

Data Handling After Removal

When you remove the app from your Zoom account, Mileo loses access to future cloud recordings. Any meeting data that was already downloaded and processed remains securely stored on your device, according to our Privacy Policy. To permanently delete your historical data, you must delete the respective meetings directly from your Mileo dashboard.
